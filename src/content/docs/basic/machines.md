The Machines section allows you to manage the physical and virtual machines in your infrastructure.

## Overview

Here you can view the status of your machines, their resource usage, and perform management actions.

## Adding a Machine

To add a new machine to your cluster:
1. Go to the Machines page.
2. Click "Add Machine".
3. Follow the instructions to provision the machine.

## Monitoring

You can monitor the health and performance of your machines directly from the dashboard.
