The Models section allows you to manage Large Language Models (LLMs) deployed on your infrastructure.

## Overview

View available models, their status, and resource consumption.

## Deploying a Model

To deploy a new model:
1. Navigate to the Models section.
2. Select "Deploy Model".
3. Choose the model type and configuration.
4. Confirm deployment.
